# Braintrust

## What it is

Braintrust is an AI evaluation and observability platform whose material in this vault focuses on how to instrument, score, and analyze multi-turn LLM applications in production.

## Why it matters here

Braintrust matters because it contributes a practical operations view of evaluation: traces, turn-scoped scoring, conversation-scoped scoring, asynchronous online evaluation, and traffic-level pattern discovery. That makes it a useful complement to the vault's more model- or retrieval-centric evaluation sources.

## Notes

- The current Braintrust source in the vault is [[Braintrust - How to evaluate multi-turn conversations]].
- In this knowledge base, Braintrust mainly strengthens the measurement side of conversational AI rather than the generation side.
- The source is especially useful because it turns evaluation into an operational stack, not just a benchmark discussion.
