# Fei-Fei Li

## Who they are

Fei-Fei Li is a professor at Stanford University and co-founder of World Labs. She is known for co-creating ImageNet (the benchmark dataset that enabled the deep learning revolution in computer vision) and for her work on spatial intelligence as AI's next frontier after language.

## Why they matter to this vault

Author of the functional taxonomy essay that deepens the vault's [[World Models]] concept page. Her framing of the POMDP loop as the unifying structure beneath renderers, simulators, and planners is the most precise treatment of world models in this vault.
