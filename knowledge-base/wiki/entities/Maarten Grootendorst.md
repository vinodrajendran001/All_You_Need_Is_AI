# Maarten Grootendorst

## What it is

Maarten Grootendorst is an AI educator and author known for highly visual explainers of LLM internals and co-author of the O'Reilly book *Hands-On Large Language Models*. In this vault he is the author of [[Maarten Grootendorst - A Visual Guide to Quantization]], a 50+ illustration walkthrough of quantization from float representation through BitNet.

## Why it matters here

His quantization guide is the most thorough standalone treatment of the numerical machinery behind [[Model Quantization and Efficiency]] that the vault has — the affine map, symmetric vs asymmetric quantization, PTQ vs QAT, and GPTQ vs GGUF. It supplies the weight-space foundation that more specialized sources (the TurboQuant KV-cache work, the prefill/decode inference post) build on or deliberately contrast against.

## Notes

- Publishes via his own Substack newsletter; the quantization guide dates to July 2024.
- Style is diagram-first and beginner-friendly, which makes his material a good "seed" reference for concept pages.
