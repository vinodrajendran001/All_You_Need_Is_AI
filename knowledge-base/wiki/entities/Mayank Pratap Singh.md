# Mayank Pratap Singh

## What it is

Mayank Pratap Singh is a writer for [[Vizuara]] who produces diagram-first explainers of vision and generative-model architectures. In this vault he authored two sources: [[Mayank Pratap Singh - Diffusion Model Visual Breakdown]] and [[Mayank Pratap Singh - Transformers for Video - TimeSformer]].

## Why it matters here

His pieces anchor two vision branches of the vault:

- **Generative vision** — the Diffusion Model Visual Breakdown seeds [[Diffusion Models]] (forward noising, denoising targets, latent diffusion, conditioning, classifier-free guidance, diffusion transformers).
- **Video understanding** — the TimeSformer explainer seeds [[Video Transformers]], extending [[Transformer Architecture]] into divided space-time attention.

Together with [[Siddhant Rai]], he makes [[Vizuara]] one of the vault's recurring explainer sources.

## Notes

- Writes pedagogical secondary coverage; durable claims trace back to the underlying papers (DDPM and TimeSformer, respectively).
