# The Pocket

## What it is

The Pocket is the GitHub organization behind PocketFlow and the PocketFlow Tutorial Video Generator documentation set. Their positioning is explicitly educational and product-oriented: **"AI Tutor for Learning — We use AI to make education addictive."**

## Why it matters here

For this vault, The Pocket matters because it is not just publishing isolated notes; it is producing a coherent tutorial stack that turns hard technical topics into modular lessons. That makes it a strong composite source for LLM internals, post-training, reinforcement learning, and mathematical foundations.

## Notes

- PocketFlow itself is their workflow framework.
- The tutorial docs show a repeatable house style: short promised time-to-understanding, first-principles explanation, concrete math, and code-level implementation detail.
- The source collection broadens this wiki beyond surveys and essays by adding structured educational material that can seed many future concept pages.
