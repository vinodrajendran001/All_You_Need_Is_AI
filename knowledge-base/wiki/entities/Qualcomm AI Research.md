# Qualcomm AI Research

## What it is

Research initiative of Qualcomm Technologies focused here on making reasoning-capable LLMs practical on mobile and edge hardware.

## Why it matters here

Qualcomm AI Research matters because it strengthens a branch that was only lightly represented before: not just local inference, but **local reasoning under mobile constraints**. Its source ties together LoRA-based reasoning adaptation, budget-aware RL, KV-cache reuse, verifier-guided parallel decoding, and quantized Android deployment.

## Notes

- The current source in the vault is [[Efficient Reasoning on the Edge]].
- In this knowledge base, Qualcomm AI Research primarily expands the on-device deployment branch rather than the general web-scale training branch.
- The paper is useful because it treats deployment as a full stack, from post-training objective design through quantization and export, instead of as a final packaging step.
