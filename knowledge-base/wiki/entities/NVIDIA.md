# NVIDIA

## What it is

Technology company and research organization whose material in this vault currently centers on high-throughput vision-language grounding.

## Why it matters here

NVIDIA matters because its LocateAnything source opens a new branch around multimodal localization, showing that inference bottlenecks and interface design also matter for grounding models, not only for text LLMs.

## Notes

- The current NVIDIA source in the vault is [[NVIDIA - LocateAnything]].
- In this knowledge base, NVIDIA currently strengthens the multimodal/perception side of agent systems more than the general LLM-training side.
- The source is especially relevant because it connects detection, grounding, GUI understanding, OCR, and document layout into one unified framing.
