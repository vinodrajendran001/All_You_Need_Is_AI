# Y Combinator

## What it is

Y Combinator is a startup accelerator and media/publishing platform that also hosts technical programming such as YC Paper Club.

## Why it matters here

In this vault, Y Combinator matters as the publisher of a research-discussion source that connects frontier AI papers to startup and operator interests. That makes it useful not just as a content source, but as a signal of which research themes were seen as practically important.

## Notes

- The YC Paper Club format is different from a single paper or tutorial: it is a curated snapshot of several fast-moving research directions in one session.
- This source adds a founder/operator lens to topics that already matter in the vault, including inference efficiency, world models, and pretraining.
