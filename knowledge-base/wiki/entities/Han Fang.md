# Han Fang

## What it is

Han Fang is the GitHub contributor behind the `pytorch-practice` repository, a compact PyTorch tutorial series built for learning and machine-learning engineer interview preparation.

## Why it matters here

This source adds a strongly practical voice to the vault's PyTorch coverage. Instead of presenting only conceptual summaries, Han Fang's tutorial uses executable scripts and from-scratch implementations to make tensor operations, autograd, training loops, normalization, attention, optimization, and efficiency techniques concrete.

## Notes

- The repository is organized as a progressive curriculum rather than a loose snippet collection.
- Its code examples emphasize interview-relevant implementation details such as stable softmax, custom BatchNorm, Focal Loss, gradient accumulation, weight initialization, and masked multi-head attention.
- The source complements broader tutorial collections in this vault by serving as a focused, runnable PyTorch practice repo.
