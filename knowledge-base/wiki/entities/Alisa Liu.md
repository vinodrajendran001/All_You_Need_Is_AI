# Alisa Liu

## What it is

Alisa Liu (publishes as **alisawuffles**) is an NLP researcher who completed a 6-year PhD at the University of Washington, with a final-stretch focus on **tokenization**. She applied for Research Scientist / Member of Technical Staff roles and documented the experience publicly. (The first name is source-backed via the `alisawuffles` handle and the `alisa-s-book-of-llms` Notion URL; the PhD/tokenization details are self-described in her job-search post.)
