# Nathan Lambert

## Who they are

Nathan Lambert is the author of Interconnects and the host/interviewer for the source [[Nathan Lambert - Frontier post-training recipe review with Finbarr Timbers]]. In this vault, he matters as a recurring explainer of RLHF, post-training, open models, and the gap between academic/open recipes and frontier-lab recipes.

## Why they matter to this vault

The Finbarr Timbers interview contributes the vault's clearest historical map of post-training recipe evolution, especially the shift from InstructGPT-style RLHF to 2026 specialist-teacher and MOPD-style frontier recipes.
