# World Labs

## What it is

World Labs is an AI company co-founded by Fei-Fei Li focused on spatial intelligence and world models. Its stated goal is to build a unified world model capable of rendering photorealistic views, simulating physically accurate environments, and planning action sequences from a single foundation model.

## Why it matters to this vault

The company is the organizational context behind the Fei-Fei Li taxonomy essay, which provides the clearest functional decomposition of world models in this vault. World Labs' product **Marble** — a multimodal-to-3D environment generator outputting Gaussian splats and collision meshes — is the vault's first concrete example of a system that dissolves the renderer/simulator boundary.
